# Techno King

He gave himself the title. The SEC investigated whether it violated disclosure rules. He kept it.

**Literal meaning**
*Techno* — technology. *King* — a hereditary monarch. Techno King is the title Elon Musk assigned himself at Tesla in 2021 — combining feudal political authority with technological power.

**Origin**
Tesla filed with the SEC in April 2021 that Musk's new title was "Techno King of Tesla." Widely interpreted as either a joke or a deliberate provocation. Analytically significant as a moment of explicit feudal-technological framing — Technofeudalism named as personal identity.

> Techno King is the explicit fusion of feudal authority and technological power as a personal title — making visible the political ambition that technofeudalism usually leaves implicit.

**The Appeal**
The irony and provocation of the title are part of its function: it performs casualness about power that makes critique seem humourless.

**The Friction**
The irony is functional: it pre-empts critique by framing concern as the absence of a sense of humour. [[terms/society_power/muskism_public|Muskism]] uses the same mechanism at scale. The title also appeared in SEC filings — it is legally documented.

**Why This Matters**
Once you see Techno King as a political statement rather than a joke, you see that naming oneself king — even ironically — is a claim about the relationship between technical and political authority.
