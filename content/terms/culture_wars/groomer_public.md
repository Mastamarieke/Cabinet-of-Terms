# Groomer

The word had a precise, serious meaning. Then it was applied to teachers, librarians, and politicians. The original meaning did not disappear. It was used as a weapon.

**Literal meaning**
*Groomer* — originally: someone who gains the trust of a child for purposes of sexual abuse. In weaponised form: applied to any person accused of "indoctrinating" children with LGBTQ+ content.

**Origin**
Groomer as a political term accelerated in US discourse in 2022. The strategy borrows a serious child safety term and applies it to political opponents, conflating sexual abuse with LGBTQ+ visibility.

> Groomer, in its weaponised political use, is the semantic capture of a child safety term — applying it to political opponents to delegitimise through association with abuse.

**The Appeal**
Child safety is a genuine and important concern. The term's original meaning carries legitimate weight.

**The Friction**
Semantic capture is the mechanism: the authority of the original term is borrowed and redirected. The people targeted are typically not engaged in child abuse — the accusation works by association, not evidence.

**Why This Matters**
Once you see Groomer as semantic capture, you see how the weaponisation of protective language is itself a form of harm.
