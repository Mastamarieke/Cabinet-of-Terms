# Deep State

The term has a legitimate meaning. It also has a different meaning. Both circulate simultaneously. This is not an accident.

**Literal meaning**
*Deep* — below the surface, permanent. *State* — the governmental apparatus. Academic usage: the permanent bureaucratic apparatus. Populist usage: a secret elite controlling governments.

**Origin**
Deep State as an academic concept describes the permanent civil service apparatus. The term was appropriated in US political discourse from around 2017, shifted from structural description to conspiracy theory.

> Deep State is a term with two meanings — a legitimate academic concept and a conspiracy theory — whose conflation is itself a rhetorical strategy.

**The Appeal**
The legitimate concern about permanent bureaucratic power is real. Elected governments do face institutional resistance.

**The Friction**
Semantic capture: the legitimate term is borrowed to give authority to the conspiracy theory version. [[terms/culture_wars/qanon_public|QAnon]] uses Deep State as its central explanatory concept.

**Why This Matters**
Once you see Deep State as a semantic capture, you see that the conflation allows conspiracy thinking to borrow the credibility of legitimate political analysis.
