# Wellness-to-Alt-Right Pipeline

The entry point was gluten. The exit point was the Great Replacement. The algorithm built the bridge.

**Literal meaning**
*Wellness* — health and self-care practices. *Alt-Right* — far-right political ideology. *Pipeline* — a pathway through which people move. The documented trajectory from wellness communities to far-right ideology.

**Origin**
The pipeline was documented by journalists and researchers from around 2018. Research traced how anti-vaccination, anti-pharmaceutical wellness content connected algorithmically to far-right political content through shared enemy frameworks.

> The Wellness-to-Alt-Right Pipeline is the documented algorithmic pathway connecting wellness and spirituality communities to far-right political ideology — through the shared framework of anti-establishment distrust.

**The Appeal**
Health scepticism and distrust of pharmaceutical companies are responses to genuine failures of medical institutions.

**The Friction**
