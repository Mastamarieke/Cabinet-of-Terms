# Woke Mind Virus

It spreads like a virus. It infects institutions. The cure is exposure. This is not a metaphor — or rather, that is exactly what it is.

**Literal meaning**
*Woke* — originally African American slang for social awareness; appropriated. *Mind Virus* — a belief system that propagates like a pathogen. The compound frames progressive political views as a contagious disease.

**Origin**
Elon Musk popularised "woke mind virus" on Twitter/X from 2022. The term builds on earlier pathologisation of progressive politics that accelerated in online spaces from 2015.

> Woke Mind Virus is the framing of progressive political convictions as a contagious pathogen — using medical language to pathologise and delegitimise political positions.

**The Appeal**
For people who feel that progressive cultural norms are being imposed on institutions they valued, the virus metaphor names a real experience of rapid change and perceived loss.

**The Friction**
[[terms/culture_wars/social_contagion_public|Social Contagion]] is the underlying rhetorical structure. Medical framing has political consequences: if a belief is a virus, holders are infected rather than persuaded. [[terms/platform_mechanisms/ragebaiting_public|Ragebaiting]] amplifies the term because it provokes strong reactions across the political spectrum.

**Why This Matters**
Once you see "woke mind virus" as a rhetorical strategy, you see that the medical metaphor removes the possibility of legitimate political disagreement and substitutes a pathology narrative.
