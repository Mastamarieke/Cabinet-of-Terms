# QAnon

It began on an imageboard. It spread to Facebook groups. It reached parliaments.

**Literal meaning**
*Q* — the anonymous figure who posted coded messages. *Anon* — anonymous. QAnon is the conspiracy theory that a secret elite of Satan-worshipping paedophiles controls global politics.

**Origin**
QAnon began on 4chan in October 2017. By 2021, QAnon beliefs had been documented among elected officials in multiple countries and were cited by participants in the January 6 US Capitol attack.

> QAnon is the conspiracy theory that migrated from imageboard subculture to mainstream political discourse through platform amplification — integrating existing conspiracy frameworks under a single narrative architecture.

**The Appeal**
QAnon offers community, a coherent explanatory framework for confusing events, and the appeal of special knowledge.

**The Friction**
[[terms/platform_mechanisms/recommender_systems_public|Recommender Systems]] accelerated QAnon's spread: the theory is designed to generate engagement through mystery, revelation, and community. [[terms/culture_wars/deep_state_public|Deep State]] is its central structural concept.

**Why This Matters**
Once you see QAnon as a platform-amplified conspiracy architecture, you see that the distribution infrastructure that allowed it to reach mainstream audiences is still operational.
