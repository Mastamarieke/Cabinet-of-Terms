# Beauty, Influencers & Self-Image


***The filter makes your skin smoother, your eyes larger, your nose smaller. It takes one second. The comparison it produces lasts longer.***

This cluster maps the platform economy of appearance — how body norms are distributed as aspiration content, how comparison becomes ambient, and what happens at the clinical end of that process.

The question this cluster asks: whose standard of beauty are you measuring yourself against?

---

## Terms in this cluster

### [[Fitfluencer]]
Fitness as content. The body as platform. The workout as product.

### [[That Girl]]
Up at 5am. Green smoothie. Journalling. A lifestyle aesthetic that performs wellness without requiring it.

### [[Clean Girl Aesthetic]]
Minimalist beauty as norm. No-makeup makeup that takes forty minutes. Effortlessness as effort.

### [[Beauty Filter]]
Realtime facial adjustment. A version of yourself that only exists on screen — and that you are now measured against.

### [[Influencer]]
The word sounds neutral. The power structure inside it does not.

### [[Deinfluencing]]
Telling people to buy less — on the platform that profits from buying more.

### [[Highlight Reel]]
Everyone shows their best. You compare your worst to their best. The platform rewards this.

### [[Curated Life]]
Life as editorial product. Every meal, trip, and emotion reviewed before posting.

### [[Comparison Culture]]
Permanent, ambient comparison. Not a personality trait — a platform feature.

### [[FOBO]]
Fear of Better Options. The paralysis that comes from infinite choice — and the inability to commit to any of them.

### [[Toxic Positivity]]
The obligation to frame everything as growth. Grief as a learning opportunity. Failure as a gift.

### [[Main Character Energy]]
Framing yourself as the protagonist of your own story — and everyone else as supporting cast.

### [[Body Dysmorphic Disorder]]
The face in the mirror does not match the face in your mind. You have checked seventeen times today. This is the clinical endpoint of the aesthetic optimisation continuum.
