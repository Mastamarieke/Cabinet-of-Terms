***Your neck hurts. Your fingers cramp. You feel a vibration that is not there. You cannot remember a phone number you used to know by heart. This is normal now.***

This cluster maps what digital systems leave behind — in the body and in the brain. Not extreme cases. Ordinary use. The harm that has no name because it became the baseline.

These terms are design outcomes, not personal failures. The posture, the anxiety, the compulsion, the memory gap — each of them has a cause that precedes the individual. Understanding that cause is the first step toward holding the right thing accountable.

The question this cluster asks: what has changed in you that you have not noticed yet?

---

## Terms in this cluster

### [[Text Claw]]
Repetitive strain in fingers and hands from sustained smartphone use. A physiotherapist's term for a platform's design outcome.

### [[Tech Neck]]
Cervical spine strain from hours of looking down at a screen. The posture that became the default.

### [[Nomophobia]]
The anxiety of being without your phone. Not a character flaw — a dependency that was designed in.

### [[Digital Amnesia]]
The reduced ability to remember information you know can be looked up. Kaspersky named it in 2015. It has been getting worse since.

### [[Technostress]]
Stress caused by using or dealing with technology. Documented across all age groups. Rarely treated as a design failure.

### [[Deskilling]]
Automation making foundational skills redundant. The tool does it better. The skill atrophies. One day the tool is unavailable.

### [[Digital Hoarding]]
The compulsive accumulation of files, messages, and screenshots combined with an inability to delete them. Unlimited storage removed the pressure to choose. *(new V10)*

### [[Digital Overload]]
The volume and pace of digital information exceeding the capacity to process it. Not a productivity problem — a design outcome. *(new V10)*

### [[Wexting]]
Walking while texting. Pedestrian infrastructure now adapts to the phone rather than the other way around. *(new V10)*

### [[Cyberchondria]]
Health anxiety escalated through repeated online symptom searching. The algorithm surfaces alarming results. The loop continues. *(new V10)*
