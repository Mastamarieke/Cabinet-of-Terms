***Words that used to mean one thing now mean another. The change was not accidental.***

This cluster maps terms that frame political positions as medical threats, hostile infiltration, or biological facts. The vocabulary here does not describe disagreements — it terminates them. When your opponent is a virus, a groomer, or subhuman, there is nothing left to discuss. The language forecloses the debate it appears to be part of.

What this cluster makes visible: the mechanism by which ordinary political conflict is transformed into something that justifies extraordinary responses.

The question this cluster asks: what kind of response does this language make seem reasonable?

---

## Terms in this cluster

### [[Anti-Woke]]
Reactive counter-force entirely defined by what it opposes. Remove the target and the identity dissolves.

### [[Conspirituality]]
Portmanteau of conspiracy and spirituality. Wellness as an on-ramp to extremist ideology.

### [[Deadnaming]]
Old names remain findable. The right to be forgotten collides with digital permanence — and identity is in the middle.

### [[Deep State]]
Two incompatible definitions sharing one term: permanent bureaucracy (academic) and secret elite (populist). The confusion is useful.

### [[Dehumanization]]
Bandura's mechanism: framing people as less than human to remove the inhibition against harming them.

### [[Deplatforming]]
Removal from platforms as a countermeasure. Effective at reducing reach. Contested as a solution.

### [[Doxing]]
Publishing private data to intimidate or harm. A weapon that requires no technical expertise — only motivation. *(new V10)*

### [[Great Replacement]]
Conspiracy theory migrated from niche forums to mainstream political discourse. The distribution infrastructure changed faster than the content.

### [[Groomer]]
Semantic capture: a child safety term repurposed as a political weapon. The original meaning does not disappear — it is borrowed.

### [[Purity Culture]]
Obsession with the pure body as political metaphor. The logic connects clean eating, anti-vaccination, and ethnonationalism.

### [[QAnon]]
Conspiracy theory as platform phenomenon. Began on an imageboard, reached parliaments.

### [[Red Pill]]
Ideological awakening framed as revelation. The metaphor does the work of making the ideology feel inevitable.

### [[Social Contagion]]
Medical vocabulary applied to transgender identity. The scientific framing lends authority the evidence does not support.

### [[Subhuman]]
Explicit dehumanisation language. Every historical instance of its use preceded something worse.

### [[Wellness-to-Alt-Right Pipeline]]
Documented pathway from health communities to extremist ideology. The algorithm built the bridge.

### [[Woke Mind Virus]]
Political conviction framed as contagion. If a belief is a virus, its holders are infected — not persuaded.
