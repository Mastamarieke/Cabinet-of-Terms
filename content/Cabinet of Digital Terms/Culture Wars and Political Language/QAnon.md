# QAnon


***It began on an imageboard in 2017. By 2021, it had reached parliaments.***

**Literal meaning:** **QAnon** is a conspiracy theory originating in anonymous posts on 4chan from October 2017, claiming that a secret cabal of Satan-worshipping paedophiles — including prominent politicians and celebrities — controls world events, and that a military insider known as "Q" is revealing this truth to prepare a group of followers for a great awakening and reckoning.

**Origin:** "Q" began posting on 4chan in October 2017, claiming to be a high-level US government insider with Q-level security clearance. The posts — called "Q drops" — were deliberately cryptic, inviting followers to decode their meaning. Researchers Kate Starbird, Renée DiResta, and others have documented how **QAnon** spread from fringe imageboards through Facebook groups, **YouTube** channels, and Twitter, reaching millions of followers globally by 2020. The January 6, 2021 storming of the US Capitol included documented **QAnon** participants. Variants of the theory have spread internationally.

> A conspiracy framework that turned political paranoia into participatory decoding — beginning on an imageboard in 2017 and reaching parliaments by 2021.

**The Appeal:** **QAnon** offers participation, not just belief. Followers are not passive consumers — they are researchers decoding hidden truths. The community is real, the social bonds are genuine, and the sense of mission is intense. It also provides a totalising explanation: once you accept the framework, everything confirms it. Confusing world events, institutional failures, and personal anxieties all become legible through the Q lens.

**The Friction:** The participatory structure is the mechanism. [[Red Pill]] epistemology — division of the world into those who see and those who are deceived — is the prerequisite. [[Deep State]] provides the institutional target. [[Recommender Systems]] accelerated the spread: **YouTube**'s recommendation algorithm was documented surfacing increasingly extreme content to users who engaged with adjacent material. [[Conspirituality]] is the adjacent pathway: wellness communities became entry points through shared distrust of institutions. The theory is unfalsifiable by design: failed predictions are reinterpreted as disinformation or tests. The January 6 connection makes **QAnon** one of the clearest cases of online radicalisation producing real-world political violence.

**Why This Matters:** **QAnon** makes visible the full arc of digital radicalisation: from anonymous imageboard post to parliamentary storming in four years. The speed is new. The structure — hidden elite, awakened community, imminent reckoning — is ancient.
