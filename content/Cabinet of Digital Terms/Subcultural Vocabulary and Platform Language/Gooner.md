# Gooner


***Compulsive fixation as public vocabulary. A private behaviour named, shared, and made into identity.***

**Literal meaning:** A **gooner**, in its original and still primary sense, describes someone engaged in extended, compulsive consumption of pornographic content — a portmanteau of "goon" (to be in a dissociated, trance-like state) and the "-er" suffix denoting a practitioner. In broader internet use, it has been extended to describe any intense, compulsive fixation: someone who is obsessively devoted to a game character, media franchise, or aesthetic.

**Origin:** The term originated in online pornography communities and spread into broader internet culture from around 2022–2023, primarily through **TikTok**, where it was adopted ironically and non-sexually — "gooning" over a video game character, a food item, or an aesthetic. The ironic adoption created distance from the term's original context while preserving its structure: compulsive fixation as identity.

> A term for compulsive fixation that migrated from private pornographic community usage into mainstream ironic internet culture — naming a psychological state (dissociated compulsive consumption) and making it public, shared, and funny.

**The Appeal:** The ironic adoption of "**gooner**" is an example of internet culture's characteristic move of taking transgressive vocabulary and making it shareable through irony. It names a genuinely recognisable experience — the state of being deeply, stupidly fixated on something — with enough distance (through irony) to make it comfortable to share.

**The Friction:** The term's migration from private compulsive pornography consumption to ironic general fixation does not erase its origin context — it imports the structure of that context into new applications. [[Dopamine Feedback Loops]] — variable reward schedules — are the psychological mechanism: goon states are dissociated states produced by compulsive engagement with high-stimulation content. [[Gaming Disorder]] is the clinical cousin: compulsive consumption of gaming content that produces similar dissociated states. [[Cognitive Surrender]] — the surrender of reflective capacity to digital systems — is what gooning describes phenomenologically. The ironic register makes the state comfortable to discuss while potentially normalising it.

**Why This Matters:** **Gooner** makes visible how a clinical phenomenon (compulsive dissociated consumption) can be named, shared, and made into identity — and how irony is the mechanism that enables the transition from pathology to subcultural vocabulary.
