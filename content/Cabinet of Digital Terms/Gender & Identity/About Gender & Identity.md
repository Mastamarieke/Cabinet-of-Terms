# Gender & Identity


***Somewhere online, attractiveness is being turned into a number. And that number is being used to rank people.***

This cluster maps the vocabulary of the manosphere and its adjacent spaces — communities that distribute normative gender hierarchies as personal philosophy. These terms did not emerge from nowhere. They are the product of specific platforms, specific algorithms, and specific grievances that the internet learned to amplify.

The question this cluster asks: what happens when gender becomes a market?

---

## Terms in this cluster

### [[Looksmaxxing]]
Optimising your face and body as a project with measurable outcomes. The logic of the gym applied to the mirror — and then to everything else.

### [[Manosphere]]
Not one community but many — connected by a shared belief that men are losing something. The platforms made the connections possible. The algorithms made them loud.

### [[Womanosphere]]
The female-facing mirror of the manosphere. Same SMV logic, different costume. High value women, hypergamy, female strategy — packaged as empowerment.

### [[Tradwife]]
Domesticity as identity project, distributed on Instagram. The return to the 1950s housewife — performed, monetised, and algorithmically amplified.

### [[Provider]]
The male caretaker role reframed as a transaction. You provide. She stays. The relationship as contract.

### [[Top 1% Man]]
A category that is always just out of reach. The aspirational ceiling that keeps men consuming self-improvement content indefinitely.

### [[Alpha Male]]
A term borrowed from wolf behaviour research that turned out to be wrong. The science was corrected. The ideology kept going.

### [[Sigma Male]]
The loner who wins by refusing to play. A status symbol for people who say status doesn't matter to them.

### [[High Value Man]]
Self-development repackaged as sexual strategy. The market logic applied to the self: invest, improve, yield returns.

### [[Stay-at-home Girlfriend (SAHG)]]
Financial dependence without marriage. A relationship model promoted as freedom — on platforms built by investors.

### [[Rizz]]
Charisma as a skill you can acquire. The dating coach industry distilled into one word.

### [[UNC]]
The boring adult who doesn't get it. A label that keeps older people outside the conversation.

### [[NPC]]
People on autopilot, without real inner lives. A gaming term that became a way to dehumanise people you disagree with.

### [[Incel]]
Involuntary celibate — a community built around resentment. Started as a support space. Became something else.

### [[Blackpill]]
The belief that your attractiveness is fixed by biology and there is nothing you can do. Determinism as identity.

### [[MGTOW]]
Men Going Their Own Way — withdrawal from relationships with women as a political position.

### [[SMV (Sexual Market Value)]]
Attractiveness quantified. Your worth on a scale of one to ten, updated in real time.

### [[Hypergamy]]
The claim that women always want to date above their station. A theory that explains every rejection without requiring self-reflection.

### [[Podcast-bro]]
The male podcaster as ideological gatekeeper. Three hours, no editor, millions of listeners.
