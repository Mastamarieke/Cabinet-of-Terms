# Conspirituality

It started with essential oils and ended with election fraud theories. The algorithm connected the dots.

**Literal meaning**
*Conspiracy* — a secret plot. *Spirituality* — personal practices of meaning. Conspirituality is the fusion of conspiracy thinking and wellness/spirituality culture.

**Origin**
Sociologists Charlotte Ward and David Voas coined conspirituality in a 2011 paper. It gained renewed attention during COVID-19, when anti-vaccination beliefs spread through wellness communities.

> Conspirituality is the fusion of wellness, spirituality, and conspiracy thinking — a pathway documented in research and platform data that leads from health communities to extremist ideology.

**The Appeal**
Wellness and spirituality communities address genuine needs: meaning, community, and scepticism of pharmaceutical and institutional authority.
