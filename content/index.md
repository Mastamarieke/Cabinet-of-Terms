# Kabinet van Digitale Termen

*Project Digitale Alertheid — HAN CMD — 2026*
*Vault versie: v7 — april 2026 — 160 van 246 termen klaar*

---

## What is this?

The Kabinet van Digitale Termen is a knowledge base of terms circulating in digital media, the platform economy, political discourse, and popular culture — each of which conceals something about the systems it is part of.

This is not a glossary. A glossary defines. This collection investigates — tracing terms back to their origin, their use, their significance, and the shifts in meaning they carry.

---

## How it works

Each term has a **public entry** — a short, accessible text (maximum 300 words) that makes the term recognisable and shows what it conceals. Some terms also have a **deep dive** — a full analytical study examining etymology, genealogy, power structures, and societal impact.

The terms are organised into **21 clusters** — analytical groupings that show how terms relate to each other. The graph view makes these relationships visible: causes, mechanisms, consequences, reactions.

---

## Method

This vault was built using **cartographic prompting** — a research method developed within Project Digitale Alertheid at HAN CMD.

Prompts: Media Cartografie V83 (deep dives) · Lumo V13 (public entries)
Term source: Term Seeker V8 · 246 terms · 21 clusters
